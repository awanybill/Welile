About Us

What We See

At Welile, we see that much of Africa’s valuable data isn’t online — yet. But once it is, that data can unlock real financial value for individuals, businesses, and the global economy. That’s what we’re building: a system that brings Africa’s offline data online to create opportunity.

What We’re Doing Now

We help tenants access rent support by collecting and organizing their data. This allows us to match them with Tenant Partners who are ready to fund rent fees — creating a seamless rent payment experience for all.

Financial Value Creation

Our Tenant Partners earn passive income by funding rent payments through our platform. It's a win-win system: tenants get the help they need, and partners get steady, reliable returns.
